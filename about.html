About Me

Hi! I'm Tony Capps, a cybersecurity enthusiast and aspiring ethical hacker. I started this site to share ideas, build helpful tools, and raise awareness about how everyone—from individuals to businesses—can protect themselves online.

When I'm not working on security scripts or websites, I enjoy tech reviews, gaming, and learning about open-source intelligence tools. I believe in teaching by doing, and this website is one of the ways I'm putting that belief into practice.
